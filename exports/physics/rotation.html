1 Common formulae

1.1 Rotational Inertia through central axis of hoop

\[ I = MR^2 \]

1.2 RI through central axis of an annular cylinder

\[ I = \frac{1}{2}M(R_1^2+R_2^2) \]

1.3 Solid cylinder about central axis

\[ I = \frac{1}{2}MR^2 \]

1.4 Solid cylinder about central diameter

\[ I = \frac{1}{2}MR^2 + \frac{1}{12}ML^2 \]

1.5 Thin rod about central diameter

\[ I = \frac{1}{12}ML^2 \]

1.6 Solid cylinder about diameter

\[ I = \frac{2}{5}MR^2 \]

1.7 Thin spherical shell about any diameter

\[ I = \frac{2}{3}MR^2 \]

1.8 Hoop about diameter

\[ I = \frac{1}{2}MR^2 \]

1.9 Slab about center

\[ I = \frac{1}{12}M(l^2 + w^2) \]
